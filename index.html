Indoor House Plants

Beginner-Friendly

Pothos

Epipremnum Aureum

Golden Pothos

The Pothos, or the Epipremnum aureum, is native to Southeast Asia. It has the reputation of being one of the easiest houseplants to take care of. Its common name, Pothos, comes from the genus it was once classified under.

Sunlight Requirements: Medium to low indirect light
 
Water Requirements: Water every 1-2 weeks
 
Temperature: 65°F-85°F range (18°C-30°C)

Monstera

Monstera Deliciosa

Monstera Deliciosa

Monstera are species of evergreen tropical vines and shrubs that are native to Central America. They are famous for their natural leaf-holes, which has led to the rise of their nickname, Swiss Cheese Plant.

Sunlight Requirements: Bright indirect to medium light
 
Water Requirements: Water every 1-2 weeks
 
Temperature: 65°F-85°F range (18°C-30°C)

Peperomia

Peperomia Obtusifolia

Peperomia

The Peperomia obtusifolia, also known as the Baby Rubber Plant, is an easy-going houseplant characterized by its thick, succulent-like green leaves. Peperomia are are generally easy houseplants to grow.

Sunlight Requirements: Medium to bright indirect light
 
Water Requirements: Water every 1-2 weeks
 
Temperature: 65°F to 75°F range (18°C-30°C)

Snake Plant

Dracaena Trifasciata

Snake Plant

The Snake Plant is a succulent plant characterized by its sword-like leaves with vibrant yellow edges. It is popular for its easy-going nature—it can tolerate drought and lower light—and its air-purifying capabilities.

Sunlight Requirements: Bright indirect to low light
 
Water Requirements: Water every 2–3 weeks
 
Temperature: 65°F to 75°F range (18°C-30°C)
